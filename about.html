About Witch Audio

Witch Audio is a personal website, currently in progress. The goal is to have a space dedicated to learning web development. This site combines elements of Lo-fi, programming, and art.

As I continue to learn more, I will keep updating this site. In the meantime, there will be bugs and features that are not fully supported or finished.
